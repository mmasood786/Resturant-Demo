Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use my gift card?

You can use your gift card to order from our menu online, via WhatsApp, or in-store. Simply provide your gift card code when placing your order, and the amount will be deducted from your balance.

Can I reload my gift card?

Currently, gift cards cannot be reloaded. However, you can purchase a new gift card with any amount between $10 and $500.

Do gift cards expire?

Unless otherwise stated at the time of purchase, our gift cards do not expire. Check your card details above to confirm whether your card has an expiration date.

What if my balance is insufficient?

If your order exceeds your gift card balance, you can pay the difference using another payment method (cash, card, etc.). Just let us know when placing your order.
