Our Story

From Tokyo to your table—a journey of passion and perfection

Born in Tokyo, Perfected for You

Sushi Warriors was born from a simple dream: to bring authentic Japanese flavors to our community. In 2011, Chef Tanaka left his prestigious position at a Michelin-starred Tokyo restaurant to open our doors.

Every technique, every recipe, every presentation has been refined over decades. Our fish is sourced from the same trusted suppliers at Tsukiji Market, and our rice is seasoned with a family recipe passed down through three generations.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 happy customers annually, but our mission remains the same: every plate should be a masterpiece.

Meet Chef Tanaka

With over 25 years of experience, Chef Tanaka is the heart and soul of Sushi Warriors. Trained in the rigorous traditions of Edomae sushi, he apprenticed for 8 years before earning the title of Itamae (sushi master).

His philosophy is simple: "Respect the ingredient, honor the tradition, delight the guest."

When he's not in the kitchen, Chef Tanaka travels across Japan discovering regional specialties and bringing fresh inspiration back to our menu.

"Cooking is not just about feeding people. It's about creating moments of joy."
